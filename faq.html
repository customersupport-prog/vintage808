01 — Orders
Orders & Payments

Browse our shop, select your size and add items to your cart. When you're ready, click the cart icon and proceed to checkout. You can check out as a guest or create an account for faster future orders and order tracking.

We accept the following payment methods via PayFast, our secure payment partner:

  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Instant EFT
  • SnapScan
  • Zapper
  • Mobicred

Your card details are never stored on our servers. All payments are processed securely by PayFast.

We begin processing orders quickly, so changes or cancellations must be requested within 1 hour of placing your order. Contact us immediately at customersupport@vintage808info.com with your order number.

Once an order has been dispatched, it can no longer be cancelled. You are welcome to return it once received in accordance with our Returns Policy.

Please check your spam or junk folder first — confirmation emails sometimes end up there. If you still can't find it, contact us at customersupport@vintage808info.com with the email address used at checkout and we'll confirm your order status.

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, provided items are unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached. For full details, please read our Returns Policy.

Email us at customersupport@vintage808info.com with your order number and the reason for the return. Our team will guide you through the process and provide a return address.

04 — Products
Products & Sizing

Each product page includes a size guide with measurements. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to the size chart rather than going by your usual label size, as fits can vary between styles.

If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit.

Always follow the care label instructions on the garment. As a general guide for our pieces:

  • Machine wash cold (30°C) on a gentle cycle
  • Turn garments inside out to preserve print quality
  • Do not tumble dry — air dry flat or hang
  • Do not iron directly on printed areas

Some items are limited runs and may not be restocked. Follow us on Instagram and X to be the first to know about restocks and new drops. You can also contact us to be added to a waitlist for specific items.
